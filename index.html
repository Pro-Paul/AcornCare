The Acorn Care way!

Acorn Care offer assessment, evaluation, individualized intervention, and advocacy on behalf of children, and families in Hong Kong. We help clients to achieve academic and personal success. offering professional advice after consulting with the significant members of the client's world. ​
‍
Acorn Care consultants are well versed in formal and informal assessment to best identify the strengths and challenges of the client.  For follow up, we offer a wide range of learning methods and strategies, as well as signposting to relevant services in the community.​

At Acorn Care, we offer our services to families who need quality additional support at a price tag that is realistically affordable.
